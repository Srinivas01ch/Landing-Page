FAQ

Questions answered

What kind of photo should I upload?
+
A clear, front-facing photo of your child's face works best — good lighting, no heavy shadows. A simple phone photo is perfect. Our AI will composite your child's face into every page of the book. Avoid group photos or photos where the face is partially hidden.
Can I write the child's name in Telugu?
+
Yes! At checkout you can enter the child's name in both English and Telugu. The English name appears on every page header, and the Telugu name is optionally shown in the Telugu sentence strip at the bottom. If you skip the Telugu name, we use the English name everywhere.
How long does delivery take?
+
Standard delivery: 5–7 working days across India. Express delivery (add-on): 2–3 working days. We currently deliver across all major Indian cities. After your order is placed, you will receive a tracking link via WhatsApp/SMS.
What is the book format and quality?
+
All books are printed in A5 landscape format (148mm × 210mm) — perfect for small hands. We use high-quality 170gsm matte paper for pages and a laminated cover. Each book is 26 pages (some themes have more), saddle-stitched and durable for young readers.
Can I add a personal message from me to the child?
+
Absolutely — this is one of the most loved features. At checkout there is a "Gifter's message" field where you can write a personal message (up to 120 characters) from you to the child. This message appears on the Dedication page (page 2 of every book).
Do you deliver outside India?
+
Currently we deliver within India only. International delivery (NRI families in the US, UK, UAE, Singapore, Australia) is coming in Phase 2. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when international shipping launches.
